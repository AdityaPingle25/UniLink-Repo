Frequently Asked Questions

What is UniLink? +

UniLink is a centralized platform where students can access announcements, events, internships, hackathons, and academic deadlines in one place.

How do students register for events? +

Students can browse upcoming events in the Events section and register directly through the UniLink portal.

Where can I find internship opportunities? +

Internship opportunities are listed under the Internships section where faculty and companies post available openings.

Does UniLink remind students about deadlines? +

Yes. UniLink provides automatic reminders for assignment deadlines, events, and other academic activities.

Can students participate in hackathons through UniLink? +

Yes. The Hackathons section provides information about upcoming national and international hackathons students can join.
