01

Information We Collect

When you register and use UniLink, we may collect the following types of information:

Personal Details

Full name, email address, PRN (Permanent Registration Number), department, year of study, and role (Student or Teacher).

Device Information

Browser type, operating system, IP address, and device identifiers used for accessing our platform.

Usage Data

Pages visited, features used, interaction timestamps, and general usage patterns to improve our services.

02

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To create and manage your student or teacher profile on the platform.
  • Communication: To send you announcement emails, assignment notifications, and deadline reminders via our Brevo email integration.
  • Platform Improvement: To analyze usage trends and continuously improve the user experience.
  • Event Coordination: To facilitate RSVP tracking, event registrations, and internship applications.
  • Support: To respond to your queries and technical support requests.
03

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices:

Encrypted & Protected

All passwords are hashed using bcrypt encryption before storage. Our database is hosted on MongoDB Atlas with enterprise-grade security, including TLS/SSL encryption for all data in transit and at rest.

While we implement robust safeguards, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We continuously review and update our security measures to protect your personal information.

04

Third-Party Services

UniLink integrates with the following third-party services to deliver its features:

  • Brevo (formerly Sendinblue): Used for dispatching email notifications, announcements, and deadline reminders to students.
  • MongoDB Atlas: Cloud database service for storing platform data securely.
  • Render / Hosting Providers: Server hosting and deployment infrastructure.

These services have their own privacy policies and data handling practices. We recommend reviewing their respective policies.

05

Cookies & Tracking

UniLink uses localStorage and session-based tokens for authentication purposes. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. Essential cookies may be used to:

  • Maintain your login session across pages.
  • Remember your preferences and role selection.
  • Ensure secure access to dashboard features.
06

Your Rights

As a UniLink user, you have the right to:

Access your personal data stored on the platform
Correct inaccurate or outdated information
Request deletion of your account and associated data
Export a copy of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact your department coordinator or the system administrator.

07

Children's Privacy

UniLink is designed for university students and faculty members. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 16. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate action.

08

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When changes are made, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
