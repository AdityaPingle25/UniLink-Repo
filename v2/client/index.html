Frequently Asked Questions

Need help? Here are some of our most common support questions.

Navigate to the "Internships & Hackathons" tab from your dashboard. You can filter opportunities by type, and click the "Apply Now" button on any listing to be redirected to the official application form.

Please check your Spam or Junk folder, as automated emails from our Brevo integration might sometimes be filtered. Verify that the email address linked to your UniLink student account is spelled correctly.

Currently, the registered Email and PRN cannot be modified manually through the student dashboard. If you need to make changes, please contact your department coordinator or the system administrator.

Teachers can log into the Teacher Dashboard, select the "Announcements" section, and fill out the details (Title, Audience, Description). An email will automatically be dispatched to all relevant students once saved.
